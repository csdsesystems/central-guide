Setting up your Pocket Centre

In order to get your Central up and running, you would need to configure Central to work better for you.

  • Head over to the Settings page

    You can find the Settings menu item at the bottom of the left sidebar.

  • Head over to the Bookings section

    The Bookings section will allow you to add rooms, room layouts and faculty roles.

    Adding a venue

    A venue is the basic unit that holds levels and rooms together. To add a venue, click the Add venue button and click Save.

    Adding a level

    A level can be likened to a floor. To add a level to a venue, click the Edit button.

    Adding a room

    A room is where you can attach a booking against. A room can only be added within a level.
