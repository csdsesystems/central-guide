Configure Courses

  • Head over to the Settings page

    You can find the Settings menu item at the bottom of the left sidebar. Click Courses.

  • Configuring Courses

    The Courses section allows you to manage custom course price types.

  • What are course price types?

    Price types allow you to set different pricing points for your Pocket Centre courses. For example, if you are running a course where some participants are getting sponsored by Company X, you can create a separate pricing type for them.

    There are exisiting default price types. You can use them for your own courses.

    NOTE: You can't edit price types of a licensed course.

    To add a custom price type, click Add custom price type.

    Enter the name and description of the price type, then click Save.

    To edit/remove a custom price type, just click Edit.
