Updating Pocket Centre details

Your Pocket Centre profile page contains relevant information about your Pocket Centre such as contact details, Pocket Centre agreement, list of equipment on loan, list of staff, website/landing page and attachments.

  • You can only update the information for Pocket Centres that you are a part of. To view all Pocket Centres, click Centres found on the left menu. Pocket Centres you are a part of are labeled "Your Pocket Centre".

    To edit a Pocket Centre, click the card or the floating button.

  • To update your Pocket Centre information, you can click on any field you want to update.

  • Pocket Centre leads

    To add or update your Pocket Centre Lead/s, click Edit.

    Select or un-select staff that you want to be Pocket Centre Lead/s. If you can't find the staff in the list, it's most likely that they have not been added to your Pocket Centre.

  • Adding a staff to your Pocket Centre

    To add a staff to your Pocket Centre, click Add staff in the Staff section.

    In the Add staff dialog, search the account of the person you want to add. The person you want to add must have a CSDS account.

  • Equipment on loan

    This section lists all equipment that are on loan to your Pocket Centre. If you need to extend an equipment's due date or transfer your equipment to someone else in your pocket, please contact us.

  • Attachments

    This section allows you to manage documents that are related to the administration of your Pocket Centre.

    If you want to upload a resource for your pocket, consider uploading a Resource instead.

  • Website

    All Pocket Centres have the option to use the (1) CSDS-provided website, (2) their own website or (3) not have a website at all.

    By default, a Pocket Centre will not have a website.

    To use the CSDS-provided website, select the Use the CSDS-provided website option. Selecting that option will enable your CSDS-provided website. Your website slug is your Pocket Centre name by default. If you want to update your website slug to something shorter, please contact us and we'll do it for you.

    If you want to add a custom description on your website, add custom text on About your Pocket Centre.

    If you have your own website, select the Use our own website option. You'll have to provide your website address so we can add it to your email footer and Pocket Centre profile.

    Important: If you are using your own website, please contact us so we can assist you in displaying your courses on your website.
